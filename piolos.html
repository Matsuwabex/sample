The Place

A garden where the
night comes alive

Nestled along Governor's Drive in Carmona, Cavite, Piolo's Hangout at Bonsai Garden Restobar is your go-to destination for good food, cold beer, and great company in a lush, relaxed atmosphere.

Whether you're unwinding after a long day, celebrating with friends, or simply craving authentic Filipino comfort food, this is the spot. Open late and always welcoming — every seat is a good seat here.

From sizzling sisig to refreshing green mango salad, the menu delivers classic Filipino flavors at prices that won't break the bank. Pair your meal with an ice-cold San Mig Light Below Zero and let the evening take care of itself.
